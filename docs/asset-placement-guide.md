# Asset Placement Guide

Only place your own original materials in these folders.

Do not place stock images, copied designs, unlicensed assets, fake client work, or AI-generated final artwork into the final portfolio structure.

## Folder map

### Final original images and files

- `public/assets/original/interactive-portfolio/`
  - put final screenshots of the website, mobile layouts, final UI captures, exported diagrams, and polished interface images here
- `public/assets/original/404-self-live2d/`
  - put final concept art, cleaned character sheets, final Live2D demo captures, and approved expression images here
- `public/assets/original/ecommerce-brand-system/`
  - put final logo files, packaging mockups, storefront visuals, final content graphics, and approved brand outputs here
- `public/assets/original/portrait-persona-system/`
  - put final edited portraits, final series selections, and approved presentation images here

### Process materials

- `public/assets/process/interactive-portfolio/`
  - put sitemap drafts, wireframes, layout tests, navigation notes, visual system experiments, and version screenshots here
- `public/assets/process/404-self-live2d/`
  - put rough sketches, collage references from your own work, layer-separated files, rigging screenshots, and expression tests here
- `public/assets/process/ecommerce-brand-system/`
  - put research boards, SKU notes, category screenshots, packaging drafts, AI workflow screenshots, and content funnel diagrams here
- `public/assets/process/portrait-persona-system/`
  - put contact sheets, moodboards, lighting notes, retouch comparisons, persona matrix files, and selection sheets here

### Export and support files

- `public/assets/exports/`
  - put exported PDFs, compressed presentation images, temporary review exports, and delivery-ready files here

## File naming suggestion

Use a clear pattern so files stay sortable:

- `projectname_type_subject_version_date`

Examples:

- `interactiveportfolio_wireframe_home_v1_2026-05-22.png`
- `404self_expression_angry_v2_2026-05-22.png`
- `brandsystem_packaging_front_v3_2026-05-22.jpg`
- `portraitpersona_contacts_minimalset_v1_2026-05-22.jpg`

## Status suggestion for Day 3 inventory

When you start importing assets, mark each one as:

- `final`
- `process`
- `maybe`
- `reject`

## Before replacing placeholders

- confirm the file is your own work
- confirm it is in the correct project folder
- confirm whether it belongs in `original` or `process`
- confirm the filename is understandable
- confirm the image is strong enough to represent the project

Please provide your original assets before we replace placeholder blocks with final images in the website.
