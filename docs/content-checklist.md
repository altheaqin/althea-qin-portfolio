# Content Checklist

Use this checklist before replacing placeholders.

## Required for every project page

- one strong cover image or screenshot from your own work
- one-line concept
- context paragraph
- objective paragraph
- your role
- selected process materials
- final outputs
- reflection

## Process material options

- sketches
- contact sheets
- wireframes
- rigging screenshots
- packaging iterations
- interface screenshots
- before and after edits
- prompt workflow screenshots
- research boards
- selection notes

## Quality check

- original source confirmed
- folder placement correct
- filename clear and searchable
- process vs final clearly separated
- anything weak marked for review instead of forced into the site
