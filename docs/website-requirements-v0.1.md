## 2. Core Portfolio Positioning / 核心定位

### 2.1 Working Concept / 工作概念

**Visual Systems for Identity, Commerce, and Interactive Storytelling**
**面向身份、商业与互动叙事的视觉系统**

**English — execution reference**

Projects may begin with personal visual experiments, photography, cultural observation, or commercial practice. Each case study should show how that source material develops into an applied system, such as:

- an interactive interface;
- a visual identity or brand system;
- a communication tool;
- a digital experience;
- a creative-technology prototype;
- design research; or
- a proof of concept.

Personal creative work must not be falsely reframed as paid or commercial work. Instead, its application should be made explicit through a credible visual system, speculative interface, interaction prototype, communication experiment, or research question.

Across the portfolio, reviewers should find evidence of:

- a distinctive personal visual language;
- clear organization of complex ideas;
- awareness of audience and context;
- an ability to connect creativity with application; and
- thoughtful use of technology as a creative tool.

**中文 — 创作说明**

我的项目可能从个人影像实验、摄影、文化观察或真实商业实践开始，但最终需要形成可以被理解和使用的系统，例如互动界面、视觉身份、品牌系统、传播工具、数字体验或创意科技原型。

个人创作不需要伪装成商业项目。更重要的是诚实说明它如何被转化为视觉系统、推测性界面、互动原型、传播实验、设计研究或概念验证。作品集整体要让评审看到我的个人视觉语言、思考与组织能力、受众意识、应用能力，以及把技术当作创作工具的方式。

### 2.2 Creator Profile / 创作者画像

**English — execution reference**

The creator is an observational, analytical, and world-building-oriented interdisciplinary artist. Her work focuses on memory, space, psychological states, natural vitality, digital systems, and the relationship between control and freedom.

Her practice often begins with a recognizable subject from the physical or digital world: an old building, a human body, a market, an abandoned amusement facility, a natural landscape, a childhood object, or an interface. She isolates, deconstructs, distorts, and reconstructs that subject to expose emotional, historical, psychological, or systemic forces that are present but not immediately visible.

This profile should guide portfolio interpretation, writing, sequencing, and design. It is not a rigid formula that every project must imitate. Each case study should use only the themes and methods that genuinely arise from its source material.

**中文 — 创作说明**

我是一位以观察、分析和世界建构为主要倾向的跨学科创作者。创作长期关注记忆、空间、心理状态、自然生命力、数字系统，以及控制与自由之间的关系。

作品通常从现实或数字世界中可辨认的对象开始，例如旧建筑、人体、市场、废弃游乐设施、自然景观、童年物件或数字界面。我会通过提取、拆解、扭曲和重组，让其中原本不可见的情绪、历史、心理或系统力量逐渐显现。

这份画像用于帮助作品集解释、写作、排序和设计，而不是要求每个项目机械地复制同一个公式。每个案例只应使用真正来自其原始材料的主题与方法。

### 2.3 Core Creative Structure / 核心创作结构

**English — execution reference**

The recurring experiential structure is:

**Recognizable reality → subtle anomaly → hidden mechanism → emotional or conceptual realization → unresolved afterimage**

When appropriate, portfolio narratives and interactions may follow this progression:

1. Establish a subject the audience can immediately recognize.
2. Introduce a restrained inconsistency, displacement, or interruption.
3. Reveal the psychological, historical, technological, or systemic mechanism beneath it.
4. Create an emotional or conceptual shift in the audience's understanding.
5. Leave interpretive space rather than closing the work with one definitive explanation.

The structure should influence pacing and interpretation, not force suspense or obscure basic project information. Admissions reviewers must still be able to identify the project's context, objective, role, process, and outcome efficiently.

**中文 — 创作说明**

反复出现的体验结构是：

**可识别的现实 → 细微异常 → 隐藏机制 → 情绪或概念上的意识转变 → 未解决的余像**

在适合的项目里，叙事和互动可以先让观众认出对象，再出现一个克制的偏移或中断，随后显露背后的心理、历史、技术或系统机制，使观众对原有对象产生新的理解，最后保留一部分开放空间。

这一结构用于塑造节奏和观看方式，不应为了制造悬念而遮蔽基本信息。招生评审仍然需要高效理解项目背景、目标、我的角色、过程与成果。

### 2.4 Core Themes / 核心主题

**English — execution reference**

The portfolio may draw connections across the following recurring themes:

1. The uncanny within familiar objects and spaces.
2. Memory as material for analysis and reconstruction, rather than simple nostalgia.
3. The visualization of invisible psychological mechanisms.
4. The relationship between spatial boundaries, safety, and oppression.
5. The conflict between perfectionist control and uncontrolled vitality.
6. Slow natural time versus accelerated technological time.
7. The observer's position and the tension between distance and empathy.
8. The construction of alternative worlds that revise the rules of reality.

Project writing must identify specific evidence for any claimed theme. Avoid assigning every theme to every work or using these terms as unsupported labels.

**中文 — 创作说明**

作品集可以在不同项目之间建立以下主题联系：熟悉物件与空间中的不安感；把记忆作为分析和重构的材料，而不只是怀旧；让不可见的心理机制变得可见；空间边界与安全、压迫之间的关系；完美主义控制与失控生命力之间的冲突；缓慢自然时间与加速技术时间的对照；观察者的位置以及距离与共情的张力；以及通过另一种世界修改现实规则。

项目文案必须用具体作品和过程证据支持这些主题，不能把所有主题都套在每件作品上，也不能把关键词当作没有依据的标签。

### 2.5 Media Roles / 媒介角色

**English — execution reference**

Each medium should be explained through what it contributes to the inquiry:

- **Photography** discovers traces of memory, time, atmosphere, and unreality already present in the physical world.
- **Drawing** represents internal states, fictional worlds, symbolic relationships, and content that is psychologically real but physically impossible.
- **Digital collage and glitch** visualize information overload, fragmented identity, corrupted memory, digital intervention, and the effects of external systems on the mind.
- **3D** transforms imagined or remembered images into spatial environments that can be entered and examined.
- **Animation** reveals how a calm or familiar surface gradually changes, fractures, or becomes unstable over time.
- **Interaction** lets the audience experience questions of control, choice, observation, participation, and system manipulation.

Case studies should explain why a medium was selected and how its properties changed the work. Medium labels alone are not sufficient evidence of interdisciplinary practice.

**中文 — 创作说明**

不同媒介不是简单并列的技能，而是在研究中承担不同作用：摄影发现现实世界中已经存在的记忆、时间、氛围与非现实痕迹；绘画表现内部状态、虚构世界、象征关系和心理真实但物理上不可能的内容；数字拼贴与 glitch 把信息过载、身份碎片、损坏的记忆、数字介入以及外部系统对心智的影响可视化；3D 将想象或记忆转化成可以进入和检查的空间；动画让平静或熟悉的表面随着时间变化、破裂或失去稳定；互动则让观众亲自面对控制、选择、观察、参与和操纵系统的问题。

案例需要说明为什么选择某种媒介，以及媒介特性如何改变作品。只列出媒介名称不足以证明跨学科实践。

### 2.6 Creative Methodology / 创作方法

**English — execution reference**

The creator's working method can be documented as:

1. Observe a real object, space, memory, interface, or psychological response.
2. Identify the actual emotional or conceptual trigger.
3. Remove irrelevant visual information.
4. Connect the subject with memory, mythology, history, technology, or future speculation.
5. Analyze the hidden mechanism behind the response.
6. Introduce a meaningful displacement or anomaly.
7. Reconstruct the material as a new visual, temporal, interactive, or spatial system.
8. Leave part of the meaning unresolved for audience projection.

Use this method as a framework for selecting process evidence. A case study should show the observations, reductions, connections, tests, and reconstruction that actually occurred; it must not retroactively invent a process to match the framework.

**中文 — 创作说明**

我的创作方法可以概括为：观察真实物件、空间、记忆、界面或心理反应；找到真正触发情绪或概念的原因；去除无关视觉信息；把对象与记忆、神话、历史、技术或未来推测连接；分析感受背后的隐藏机制；加入具有意义的错位或异常；把材料重构成新的视觉、时间、互动或空间系统；最后留下部分意义供观众投射。

这一方法可以帮助筛选过程材料，但不能在项目结束后虚构一个看似完整的过程。案例中呈现的观察、删减、连接、测试与重构都必须是真实发生过的。

### 2.7 Visual Language Vocabulary / 视觉语言词汇

**English — execution reference**

Recurring visual tendencies include:

- clear hierarchy within complex compositions;
- separation of, or a deliberate relationship between, figure and environment;
- large areas of visual breathing room;
- dramatic backlight, reflections, overexposed highlights, steam, snow light, and temporary natural illumination;
- restrained overall palettes with localized high-saturation emotional or warning signals;
- combinations of hand-drawn marks and digital errors;
- conceptually motivated glitch, distortion, RGB displacement, slicing, blur, repetition, and pixelation;
- familiar imagery interrupted by localized anomalies;
- attraction followed by discomfort; and
- open-ended narrative rather than one fixed explanation.

The website may translate these tendencies into hierarchy, spacing, color, image treatment, transitions, and interaction. It must not manufacture substitute artwork or apply every device globally. Original work remains the primary visual evidence.

**中文 — 创作说明**

反复出现的视觉倾向包括：复杂构图中仍保持清楚层级；人物与环境的分离或有意关系；大面积呼吸空间；强烈逆光、反射、过曝高光、蒸汽、雪光和短暂自然照明；整体克制但在局部加入高饱和的情绪或警示信号；手绘痕迹与数字错误并置；以概念为依据使用 glitch、扭曲、RGB 位移、切片、模糊、重复和像素化；在熟悉图像中制造局部异常；先吸引再产生不适；以及保持开放叙事而不是给出唯一解释。

网站可以把这些倾向转化为层级、留白、色彩、图像处理、转场和互动，但不能制造替代作品，也不能把所有手法全局套用。我的原创作品始终是主要视觉证据。

**English — execution reference**

Observation; deconstruction; reconstruction; memory; liminal space; uncanny familiarity; psychological visualization; world-building; glitch; digital intervention; natural vitality; artificial systems; observer perspective; spatial psychology; controlled composition; localized anomaly; alternative reality.

Keywords are internal indexing and writing aids. Public-facing copy should prefer precise, project-specific language over dense keyword lists.

**中文 — 创作说明**

观察、拆解、重构、记忆、阈限空间、熟悉事物中的诡异感、心理可视化、世界建构、glitch、数字介入、自然生命力、人工系统、观察者视角、空间心理、受控构图、局部异常、另类现实。

这些词主要用于内部整理、项目关联与写作提示。面对评审的正式文案应优先使用准确、与具体项目有关的语言，而不是堆叠关键词。

## 5. Initial Project Set / 初始项目组合

The initial portfolio contains four main projects. Their order and scope may be revised as the work develops.
初始作品集包含四个主要项目，后续可以根据完成度调整顺序与范围。

### 5.1 Project 1 — Interactive Portfolio Website / 项目一：互动作品集网站

**English — execution reference**

The website itself is a creative-technology and communication project. It must demonstrate:

- bilingual visual storytelling;
- information architecture;
- interface design;
- interaction design thinking; and
- implementation capability.

It should not feel like a standard portfolio template. Candidate visual languages include a visual archive, dreamcore, restrained glitch, a 404-like interface, fragmented memory, a quiet digital space, and interactive storytelling. These are directions for exploration, not a requirement to combine every effect.

The case study should document concept development, content architecture, wireframes, visual decisions, interaction prototypes, implementation decisions, tests, revisions, final output, and reflection.

**中文 — 创作说明**

网站本身就是第一个项目。它需要展示双语视觉叙事、信息架构、界面设计、互动思考与技术实现能力，而不是套用普通作品集模板。

可以探索视觉档案、dreamcore、克制的 glitch、404 界面、碎片记忆、安静的数字空间或互动叙事，但不需要把所有效果堆在一起。这个项目也必须留下概念、信息结构、线框、视觉选择、互动原型、开发、测试、修改与反思的完整过程。

### 5.2 Project 2 — 404 Self: Live2D Interactive Avatar / 项目二：404 Self：Live2D 互动化身

**English — execution reference**

This creative-technology project transforms the applicant's original artwork into a small but complete interactive identity prototype.

Source material may include:

- a glitch portrait;
- a 404 sketch;
- a Chinese visual collage;
- fragmented-identity concepts;
- digital-anxiety themes; and
- cultural symbols.

The final work may include blinking, breathing, facial-expression changes, subtle glitch states, interactive symbols, and layered visual states. The outcome is **not a game character**. It is an experimental narrative interface, Live2D avatar, interactive character, or digital-identity prototype.

Required process evidence:

- original artwork;
- concept development;
- character redesign;
- layer separation;
- rigging process;
- interaction logic; and
- final demonstration.

**中文 — 创作说明**

该项目从我自己的 glitch 肖像、404 草图、中文视觉拼贴、身份碎片、数字焦虑与文化符号出发，把静态作品转化成一个小而完整的互动身份原型。

眨眼、呼吸、表情变化、轻微故障状态、互动符号和分层视觉都可以成为表达手段，但目标不是制作游戏角色，而是建立一个 Live2D 化身、数字身份界面或实验叙事原型。项目页要保留原作、概念发展、角色重设计、拆层、绑定、互动逻辑和最终演示。

### 5.3 Project 3 — AI-assisted E-commerce Brand System / 项目三：AI 辅助跨境电商品牌系统

**English — execution reference**

This is a real applied commercial project based on the applicant's cross-border e-commerce experience. Its core narrative is:

**Research → Insight → Design Decision → Brand System → Content Application**

Potential evidence includes:

- market, SKU, and competitor research;
- AI-assisted brainstorming and workflow documentation;
- logo and packaging design;
- product presentation and social content;
- Amazon preparation;
- trademark-process documentation;
- supplier communication;
- data visualization;
- a research dashboard;
- a content funnel;
- a decision matrix; and
- a brand-system map.

AI must be represented as a creative assistant and research tool, not as a replacement for human judgment or authorship. Claims must be supported by real work and must not disclose confidential information without permission.

**中文 — 创作说明**

这是基于我真实跨境电商经验的应用型商业项目。核心逻辑是“研究 → 洞察 → 设计决策 → 品牌系统 → 内容应用”。项目可以呈现市场、SKU 与竞品研究，AI 辅助头脑风暴和工作流，标志、包装、产品展示、社交内容、Amazon 准备、商标流程与供应商沟通等材料，也可以加入数据可视化、研究看板、内容漏斗、决策矩阵或品牌系统图。

AI 在这里是研究与创意助手，不能被描述为替代人的设计判断。所有成果必须来自真实工作；涉及商业机密的内容需要先确认是否可以公开。

### 5.4 Project 4 — Portrait Persona System / 项目四：肖像人格系统

**English — execution reference**

This photography project must not be presented as merely “a collection of photos.” It should demonstrate a system for constructing visual identities through photography.

Initial persona groups:

1. **Minimal / Attitude** — black-and-white portraits, controlled lighting, minimal styling.
2. **Youth / Social** — colorful night portraits, fashion styling, social-media atmosphere.
3. **Cultural / Festive** — Hanfu photography, Chinese New Year portraits, traditional visual symbols.

The case study should demonstrate styling, lighting decisions, audience awareness, visual-identity construction, and content application. Process evidence should include moodboards, contact sheets, selection logic, lighting notes, retouching comparisons, and a persona matrix.

**中文 — 创作说明**

这个摄影项目不能只写成“一组照片”，而要呈现摄影如何系统地构建视觉身份。初步分为三类人格：黑白、克制且有态度的 Minimal / Attitude；彩色夜景、时尚造型与社交媒体氛围的 Youth / Social；以及汉服、新年主题与传统符号构成的 Cultural / Festive。

项目需要说明造型、灯光、受众、身份建构与内容应用的选择，并展示 moodboard、contact sheet、选片逻辑、灯光笔记、修图对比和人格矩阵。

### 5.5 Optional Project 5 — Everyday Commerce Visual Archive / 可选项目五：日常商业视觉档案

**English — execution reference**

Possible outputs include a photo essay, visual archive, map interface, or lightweight data visualization. The purpose is to connect documentary photography with consumer observation, visual culture, and everyday communication.

## 7. Visual and Interaction Principles / 视觉与互动原则

### 7.1 Desired Qualities / 期望气质

**English — execution reference**

The experience should feel:

- editorial;
- personal;
- calm;
- image-focused;
- thoughtful; and

**中文 — 创作说明**

### 7.2 Avoid / 避免事项

**English — execution reference**

Avoid:

- generic startup or SaaS styling;
- excessive neon cyberpunk aesthetics;
- meaningless animation;
- over-designed interfaces;
- effects without a storytelling purpose;
- inaccessible interactions; and

**中文 — 创作说明**

### 7.3 Dreamcore and Glitch / Dreamcore 与 Glitch 的定义

**English — execution reference**

Dreamcore means quiet, empty, memory-like, and slightly unreal. It must not become horror, arbitrary surrealism, or visual chaos.

Glitch is a narrative device. Use it to express fragmentation, memory, identity, error, or transition. Do not apply random distortion throughout the interface.

Motion and interaction must clarify hierarchy, reveal content, create narrative pacing, or support the project's concept. Every major effect should have a stated purpose and a reduced-motion alternative.

**中文 — 创作说明**

这里的 dreamcore 指安静、留白、像记忆一样、略微不真实，而不是恐怖、随机超现实或视觉混乱。Glitch 是表达碎片、记忆、身份、错误或转场的叙事工具，不是到处随机扭曲。

动画与互动应该帮助理解层级、揭示内容、控制叙事节奏或支持项目概念。重要效果都要能说明“为什么存在”，并为减少动态效果的用户准备替代方案。

## 10. Design and Development Workflow / 设计与开发流程

**English — execution reference**

The existing repository baseline uses **Vite, React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS**. Future implementation should preserve this stack and its working architecture unless the owner explicitly approves a migration. Maintain clear separation among components, pages, layouts, data, styles, documentation, original final assets, process assets, and exports.

Neutral placeholders may be used while original materials are being prepared, but they must be clearly identifiable and must never imply that unfinished or unavailable work is complete. Final public-facing site copy should support English and Chinese as defined in Section 8; internal planning notes may use Chinese when that improves speed and accuracy.

Preferred responsibilities:

- **Figma:** visual direction, wireframes, layout, interaction planning, and reviewable prototypes.
- **Codex:** implementation, React/Tailwind development, reusable components, responsive behavior, accessibility, testing, and iteration.
- **Human decision:** concept, aesthetics, original content, content selection, storytelling, ethical judgment, and final approval.

Preferred sequence:

1. Confirm concept and content inventory.
2. Define information architecture and bilingual content model.
3. Design wireframes and visual system in Figma.
4. Review and approve the core direction.
5. Implement with Codex.
6. Review content, interactions, responsiveness, and accessibility.
7. Iterate and document decisions.

Implementation must not begin until the pre-coding decisions in Section 12 have been reviewed.

**中文 — 创作说明**

现有仓库的技术基础是 **Vite、React、TypeScript 与 Tailwind CSS**。除非我明确批准迁移，后续实现应保留这套技术与已有的有效架构。组件、页面、布局、数据、样式、文档、原创最终资产、过程资产和导出文件之间要保持清楚区分。

Figma 主要负责视觉方向、线框、布局、互动规划与可审阅原型；Codex 负责 React/Tailwind 实现、可复用组件、响应式、可访问性、测试与迭代；我本人负责概念、美学、原创内容、选材、叙事、伦理判断和最终确认。

建议流程是：确认概念与素材清单 → 定义信息架构和双语内容模型 → 在 Figma 设计线框与视觉系统 → 审核核心方向 → Codex 实现 → 检查内容、互动、响应式和可访问性 → 迭代并记录决定。在第 12 节的开发前事项确认之前，不进入网站编码。
