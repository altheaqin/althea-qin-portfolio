## 1. Project Background / 项目背景

### 1.1 Applicant Context / 申请背景

**English — execution reference**

This portfolio is being prepared for 2027 Fall master's applications in Hong Kong. The applicant has a B.A. in Art Studio, with minors in Art History and Asian Studies.

Target academic and professional directions include creative communication, interactive media, AI-assisted content, creative technology, multimedia storytelling, brand content, visual communication, and portfolio-driven creative roles.

The portfolio must position the applicant as **a creator who combines visual expression, storytelling, technology, AI-assisted workflows, and real-world application**. It must not read primarily as a traditional fine-art image gallery, a commercial designer portfolio only, a generic UI/UX designer portfolio, or a startup/SaaS website template.

**中文 — 创作说明**

本作品集用于申请香港地区 2027 年秋季入学的硕士项目。申请者拥有 Art Studio 文学学士背景，同时辅修 Art History 与 Asian Studies。

作品集希望连接创意传播、互动媒体、AI 辅助内容、创意科技、多媒体叙事、品牌内容与视觉传播等方向，并支持依靠作品集申请的创意类岗位。

整体定位不是传统纯艺术图片画廊，也不应只呈现商业设计、套用通用 UI/UX 叙事，或看起来像创业公司 / SaaS 模板。它要清楚地呈现我是一位能够结合**视觉表达、叙事、技术、AI 辅助工作流与真实应用场景**的创作者。

### 1.2 Project Purpose / 网站目的

**English — execution reference**

The website must serve three purposes simultaneously:

1. Present a coherent application-ready portfolio for international admissions reviewers.
2. Demonstrate the applicant's ability to structure ideas, audiences, media, and interactions.
3. Function as a portfolio project in its own right, with documented design and development decisions.

**中文 — 创作说明**

网站既是申请材料的展示载体，也是一个独立作品。它需要帮助国际评审快速理解项目，同时展示我组织信息、理解受众、运用媒介与设计互动的能力，并保留网站从概念到实现的过程证据。

## 2. Core Portfolio Positioning / 核心定位

### 2.1 Working Concept / 工作概念

**Visual Systems for Identity, Commerce, and Interactive Storytelling**
**面向身份、商业与互动叙事的视觉系统**

**English — execution reference**

Projects may begin with personal visual experiments, photography, cultural observation, or commercial practice. Each case study should show how that source material develops into an applied system, such as an interactive interface, a visual identity or brand system, a communication tool, a digital experience, a creative-technology prototype, design research, or a proof of concept.

Personal creative work must not be falsely reframed as paid or commercial work. Instead, its application should be made explicit through a credible visual system, speculative interface, interaction prototype, communication experiment, or research question.

Across the portfolio, reviewers should find evidence of a distinctive personal visual language, clear organization of complex ideas, awareness of audience and context, an ability to connect creativity with application, and thoughtful use of technology as a creative tool.

**中文 — 创作说明**

我的项目可能从个人影像实验、摄影、文化观察或真实商业实践开始，但最终需要形成可以被理解和使用的系统，例如互动界面、视觉身份、品牌系统、传播工具、数字体验或创意科技原型。

个人创作不需要伪装成商业项目。更重要的是诚实说明它如何被转化为视觉系统、推测性界面、互动原型、传播实验、设计研究或概念验证。作品集整体要让评审看到我的个人视觉语言、思考与组织能力、受众意识、应用能力，以及把技术当作创作工具的方式。

### 2.2 Creator Profile / 创作者画像

**English — execution reference**

The creator is an observational, analytical, and world-building-oriented interdisciplinary artist. Her work focuses on memory, space, psychological states, natural vitality, digital systems, and the relationship between control and freedom.

Her practice often begins with a recognizable subject from the physical or digital world: an old building, a human body, a market, an abandoned amusement facility, a natural landscape, a childhood object, or an interface. She isolates, deconstructs, distorts, and reconstructs that subject to expose emotional, historical, psychological, or systemic forces that are present but not immediately visible.

This profile should guide portfolio interpretation, writing, sequencing, and design. It is not a rigid formula that every project must imitate. Each case study should use only the themes and methods that genuinely arise from its source material.

**中文 — 创作说明**

我是一位以观察、分析和世界建构为主要倾向的跨学科创作者。创作长期关注记忆、空间、心理状态、自然生命力、数字系统，以及控制与自由之间的关系。

作品通常从现实或数字世界中可辨认的对象开始，例如旧建筑、人体、市场、废弃游乐设施、自然景观、童年物件或数字界面。我会通过提取、拆解、扭曲和重组，让其中原本不可见的情绪、历史、心理或系统力量逐渐显现。

这份画像用于帮助作品集解释、写作、排序和设计，而不是要求每个项目机械地复制同一个公式。每个案例只应使用真正来自其原始材料的主题与方法。

### 2.3 Core Creative Structure / 核心创作结构

**English — execution reference**

The recurring experiential structure is:

**Recognizable reality -> subtle anomaly -> hidden mechanism -> emotional or conceptual realization -> unresolved afterimage**

When appropriate, portfolio narratives and interactions may follow this progression. The structure should influence pacing and interpretation, not force suspense or obscure basic project information. Admissions reviewers must still be able to identify the project's context, objective, role, process, and outcome efficiently.

**中文 — 创作说明**

反复出现的体验结构是：

**可识别的现实 -> 细微异常 -> 隐藏机制 -> 情绪或概念上的意识转变 -> 未解决的余像**

在适合的项目里，叙事和互动可以先让观众认出对象，再出现一个克制的偏移或中断，随后显露背后的心理、历史、技术或系统机制，使观众对原有对象产生新的理解，最后保留一部分开放空间。

这一结构用于塑造节奏和观看方式，不应为了制造悬念而遮蔽基本信息。招生评审仍然需要高效理解项目背景、目标、我的角色、过程与成果。

## 3. Website and Hero Direction / 网站与首屏方向

### 3.1 Selected Hero Direction / 已确定首屏方向

**English — execution reference**

The selected homepage hero direction is **Original Character + Dreamcore Signpost**. This direction combines a self-representational original character with a clear navigation system. It should feel personal, narrative-driven, slightly unreal, and easy to understand.

Previous hero options such as character plus archive folder, signpost-only navigation, and self-image plus orbiting project planets are no longer the selected direction, though useful ideas may be reused later if they support the final concept.

**中文 — 创作说明**

首页首屏方向已经确定为**“原创角色 + 梦核路牌”**。这个方案把自我形象的原创角色和清晰的导航系统结合起来，应该显得个人化、有叙事感、略微不真实，同时又容易理解。

之前讨论过的“角色 + 档案盒 / 文件夹”“单纯路牌导航”“自我形象 + 项目星球环绕”等方案不再作为最终方向，但其中有用的想法后续仍可在不偏离当前方向的前提下被吸收。

### 3.2 Hero Concept / 首屏概念

**English — execution reference**

The homepage hero should feature the user's own original character, based on sketches or original visual materials. The character represents the creator and guide of the portfolio. The character should exist inside a quiet, slightly unreal, dreamcore-like visual space.

In this space, signposts point toward the main portfolio projects, and each signpost functions as a project entrance. The hero title should be integrated into the visual composition instead of appearing as ordinary webpage text. It may appear as part of the environment, an archive label, a road sign, a quiet system message, or a spatial typography element.

**中文 — 创作说明**

首页首屏应该出现一个基于你自己的原创草稿或原创视觉素材的角色。这个角色代表你本人，也像是作品集世界中的引导者。角色所在的空间应具有安静、空旷、略微不真实的梦核感。

空间中出现路牌，每个路牌指向一个主要作品项目，也作为项目入口。首屏标题不应只是普通网页标题，而应该作为画面构成的一部分出现，可以像环境中的文字、档案标签、路牌文字、安静的系统提示，或空间里的字体构成。

### 3.3 Hero Interaction MVP / 首屏交互 MVP

**English — execution reference**

The first version of the hero should prioritize stability, clarity, and navigation over cinematic complexity. The MVP should:

1. show the original character in the hero scene;
2. show signposts for the three confirmed PDF portfolio projects;
3. allow each signpost to navigate to its corresponding project page;
4. optionally add subtle stable motion, such as slight floating movement, gentle parallax, subtle signpost hover, or slight character breathing / idle motion;
5. show the project title, one-line concept, and slight highlight or movement on signpost hover; and
6. simplify on mobile into clear stacked project entrances while preserving the character + signpost concept.

**中文 — 创作说明**

第一版首屏应优先保证稳定、清晰和导航有效，不追求复杂电影感。MVP 应该：

1. 展示原创角色；
2. 展示指向三个 PDF 核心项目的路牌；
3. 允许点击路牌进入对应项目页面；
4. 只在稳定的前提下加入轻微动效，例如轻微漂浮、轻微视差、路牌 hover 高亮、角色轻微呼吸或待机动作；
5. 鼠标悬停在路牌上时显示项目标题、一句话项目概念，以及轻微高亮或移动；
6. 移动端简化为清晰的纵向项目入口，同时保留“角色 + 路牌”的概念。

### 3.4 Hero Advanced Version / 首屏进阶版本

**English — execution reference**

Advanced hero features may be considered later only after the MVP is stable. Possible advanced features include walking or idle character animation, scroll-linked scene transition, stronger dreamcore spatial depth, signposts shifting with camera movement, animated transition from signpost to project page, and project-specific visual fragments appearing around each signpost.

Do not implement advanced animation in the first version unless explicitly requested.

**中文 — 创作说明**

进阶首屏功能只能在 MVP 稳定后再考虑。可能的进阶功能包括角色走路或待机动画、滚动联动场景变化、更强的梦核空间纵深、路牌随镜头运动发生变化、从路牌进入项目页的转场动画，以及每个路牌周围出现对应项目的视觉碎片。

除非我明确要求，否则第一版不要直接实现这些复杂动效。

### 3.5 Technical Direction for Hero / 首屏技术方向

**English — execution reference**

Recommended implementation direction:

1. Start with a Figma storyboard and visual design.
2. Then implement with React + Tailwind.
3. Use Motion for React for subtle UI animation and hover effects.
4. Use GSAP ScrollTrigger only if scroll-linked animation becomes necessary later.
5. Avoid heavy 3D or complex animation in the first version.
6. Do not use a single long AI-generated video as the hero interaction.
7. Prefer controllable layered assets, SVG / PNG / WebP elements, simple animation states, and reusable components.

**中文 — 创作说明**

推荐首屏技术路线：

1. 先在 Figma 里完成 storyboard 和视觉设计。
2. 再用 React + Tailwind 实现。
3. 轻量 hover 和 UI 动效优先使用 Motion for React。
4. 只有后续确实需要复杂滚动联动时，再考虑 GSAP ScrollTrigger。
5. 第一版避免重度 3D 或复杂动画。
6. 不要用一整段 AI 生成视频作为首屏交互主体。
7. 优先使用可控的分层素材、SVG / PNG / WebP 元素、简单动画状态和可复用组件。

## 4. Website and PDF Relationship / 网站与 PDF 的关系

### 4.1 Website Scope / 网站范围

**English — execution reference**

The website can remain expandable and may include richer content, process documentation, experiments, and future additions beyond the PDF portfolio. The website itself remains important as a creative-technology and communication project, and it can still function as a web-based case study.

**中文 — 创作说明**

网站可以保持可扩展，后续可以加入比 PDF 更完整的内容、过程记录、实验项目和新增内容。网站本身仍然重要，它可以作为创意科技与传播项目继续存在，并作为基于网页的 case study 被记录。

### 4.2 PDF Portfolio Structure / PDF 作品集结构

**English — execution reference**

Because the application PDF may be limited to around 12 pages, the PDF portfolio should be selective and concise. It should focus on **two major projects plus one smaller supporting project**:

1. 404 Self
2. AI-assisted Crystal / E-commerce Brand System
3. Portrait Persona System

The Interactive Portfolio Website is no longer one of the three confirmed PDF projects, although it remains important on the website.

**中文 — 创作说明**

由于申请 PDF 很可能限制在约 12 页以内，PDF 作品集需要更精选、更简洁。PDF 应聚焦于**两个主要项目 + 一个较小的辅助项目**：

1. 404 Self
2. AI-assisted Crystal / E-commerce Brand System
3. Portrait Persona System

Interactive Portfolio Website 不再是三个已确定进入 PDF 的项目之一，但它在网站中仍然重要。

### 4.3 12-page PDF Constraint / 12 页 PDF 限制

**English — execution reference**

The application PDF may be limited to around 12 pages, so this constraint should guide content selection and project depth. A recommended planning structure is:

1. Page 1: cover / positioning
2. Pages 2-5: 404 Self
3. Pages 6-9: AI-assisted Crystal / E-commerce Brand System
4. Pages 10-11: Portrait Persona System
5. Page 12: closing / contact / website QR / selected tools

This is a planning guideline, not a fixed final layout.

**中文 — 创作说明**

申请 PDF 可能限制在约 12 页以内，因此这个限制需要指导内容筛选和项目深度。建议的规划结构是：

1. 第 1 页：封面 / 定位
2. 第 2 到 5 页：404 Self
3. 第 6 到 9 页：AI-assisted Crystal / E-commerce Brand System
4. 第 10 到 11 页：Portrait Persona System
5. 第 12 页：结尾 / 联系方式 / 网站二维码 / 工具列表

这只是规划参考，不是最终固定排版。

## 5. Project Set / 项目组合

### 5.1 Website Project — Interactive Portfolio Website / 网站项目：互动作品集网站

**English — execution reference**

The website itself is a creative-technology and communication project. It must demonstrate bilingual visual storytelling, information architecture, interface design, interaction design thinking, and implementation capability. It should document its own sitemap, wireframes, visual direction, layout evolution, screenshots, mobile adaptation, navigation logic, and final website state.

**中文 — 创作说明**

网站本身是一个创意科技与传播项目，需要展示双语视觉叙事、信息架构、界面设计、互动设计思维和实现能力。它应记录自己的 sitemap、wireframe、视觉方向、版式演化、截图、移动端适配、导航逻辑和最终网站状态。

### 5.2 PDF Major Project 1 — 404 Self / PDF 主要项目一：404 Self

**English — execution reference**

404 Self is a major creative-technology project and must be included in the PDF. It should demonstrate personal visual language, creative technology, interactive identity, transformation from personal artwork into an applied prototype, and possible Live2D, interface, motion, or web interaction directions.

The project may develop into a Live2D avatar, digital identity interface, or experimental narrative prototype. Its case study should preserve original artwork, concept development, character redesign, layer breakdown, rigging, interaction logic, and final demo evidence.

**中文 — 创作说明**

404 Self 是一个主要的 creative-technology 项目，必须进入 PDF。它需要体现个人视觉语言、创意技术、互动身份表达、如何从个人创作转化为可应用的原型，以及可能涉及 Live2D、界面、动效或网页交互的方向。

这个项目可以发展为 Live2D 化身、数字身份界面或实验叙事原型。项目页要保留原作、概念发展、角色重设计、拆层、绑定、互动逻辑和最终演示。

### 5.3 PDF Major Project 2 — AI-assisted Crystal / E-commerce Brand System / PDF 主要项目二：AI 辅助水晶 / 跨境电商品牌系统

**English — execution reference**

This is a real applied commercial project based on the applicant's cross-border e-commerce experience. It should expand beyond packaging design into a richer applied case study. Its core narrative is:

**Research -> Insight -> Design Decision -> Brand System -> Packaging / Content Application**

Potential evidence includes product / SKU research, competitor observation, audience positioning, AI-assisted brainstorming, AI-assisted risk avoidance, brand concept, logo / visual identity, packaging structure, material and cost considerations, supplier communication, product photography, Amazon / Xiaohongshu / social content application, final packaging or mockup, reflection, and iteration.

AI must be represented as a creative assistant and research tool, not as a replacement for human judgment or authorship. Claims must be supported by real work and must not disclose confidential information without permission.

**中文 — 创作说明**

这是基于我真实跨境电商经验的应用型商业项目，需要从单纯包装设计扩展成更完整的应用型 case study。核心逻辑是：

**调研 -> 洞察 -> 设计决策 -> 品牌系统 -> 包装 / 内容应用**

项目可以呈现产品 / SKU 调研、竞品观察、用户定位、AI 辅助头脑风暴、AI 辅助风险规避、品牌概念、logo 和视觉识别、包装结构、材料与成本考虑、供应商沟通、产品摄影、Amazon / 小红书 / 社交内容应用、最终包装或 mockup、反思与迭代。

AI 在这里是研究与创意助手，不能被描述为替代人的设计判断。所有成果必须来自真实工作；涉及商业机密的内容需要先确认是否可以公开。

### 5.4 PDF Supporting Project — Portrait Persona System / PDF 辅助项目：肖像人格系统

**English — execution reference**

Portrait Persona System is a smaller supporting PDF project and should be included in concise form. It should not be presented as a general photography gallery. It should demonstrate a system for constructing visual identities through photography.

Initial persona groups may include:

1. **Minimal / Attitude** — black-and-white portraits, controlled lighting, minimal styling.
2. **Youth / Social** — colorful night portraits, fashion styling, social-media atmosphere.
3. **Cultural / Festive** — Hanfu photography, Chinese New Year portraits, traditional visual symbols.

The case study should demonstrate photography foundation, image-making ability, styling and lighting control, audience / platform awareness, and visual identity thinking. Process evidence should include moodboards, contact sheets, selection logic, lighting notes, retouching comparisons, and a persona matrix.

**中文 — 创作说明**

Portrait Persona System 是一个较小的辅助 PDF 项目，应以精简形式进入 PDF。它不应该被展示成普通摄影合集，而要体现人像摄影如何系统地构建不同视觉身份。

初步分组可以包括：

1. **Minimal / Attitude**：黑白肖像、受控光线、极简造型。
2. **Youth / Social**：彩色夜景肖像、时尚造型、社交媒体氛围。
3. **Cultural / Festive**：汉服摄影、新年主题肖像、传统视觉符号。

项目需要证明摄影基础、影像生产能力、造型和光线控制、受众 / 平台意识，以及视觉身份思维。过程证据应包括 moodboard、contact sheet、选片逻辑、灯光笔记、修图对比和人格矩阵。

### 5.5 Optional Project — Everyday Commerce Visual Archive / 可选项目：日常商业视觉档案

**English — execution reference**

Develop this project only after the confirmed core projects reach a strong standard. Potential source material includes street photography, food-stall photography, local retail scenes, and everyday consumption environments. Possible outputs include a photo essay, visual archive, map interface, or lightweight data visualization.

**中文 — 创作说明**

只有已确定的核心项目完成度足够高时，才考虑增加这一项目。它可以使用街头、食物摊位、本地零售与日常消费环境的摄影，通过摄影文章、视觉档案、地图界面或轻量数据可视化，把纪实摄影与消费观察、视觉文化和日常传播联系起来。

## 6. Content and Authorship Rules / 内容与原创规则

**English — execution reference**

All final portfolio content must be grounded in the applicant's original photographs, drawings, designs, commercial work, research, and process materials.

Do not use stock imagery as substitute portfolio work, copied portfolio templates, fake projects, invented results, invented clients, invented responsibilities, invented achievements, or AI-generated final artworks that replace the applicant's original work.

AI and coding tools may assist with organization, prototyping, layout, interaction, documentation, visual planning, and implementation. The applicant retains authorship of the concept, aesthetic decisions, content selection, narrative, and final approval.

Any claim about role, outcome, commercial impact, or process must be accurate. Clearly distinguish solo work from collaboration and speculative work from commissioned or employed work.

**中文 — 创作说明**

最终展示必须基于我的原创摄影、绘画、设计、真实商业项目、研究与过程材料。不能用素材图代替作品，不能复制模板，不能虚构项目、客户、职责、成果或成就，也不能让 AI 生成图取代我的最终原创作品。

AI 与代码工具可以帮助组织、原型、布局、互动、记录、视觉规划和实现，但概念、美学、内容选择、叙事与最终判断必须由我负责。个人完成、合作完成、推测性项目和真实委托之间都要诚实区分。

## 7. Visual and Interaction Principles / 视觉与互动原则

### 7.1 Desired Qualities / 期望气质

**English — execution reference**

The experience should feel editorial, personal, calm, image-focused, thoughtful, and experimental but controlled. The first viewport should establish the applicant's distinctive creative position through the selected Original Character + Dreamcore Signpost concept and should not resemble generic product marketing.

**中文 — 创作说明**

整体气质应当具有编辑感、个人性、安静、以图像为重点、经过思考，并且实验但克制。首屏要通过“原创角色 + 梦核路牌”的方向快速建立我的创作者身份，不能像普通产品营销页。

### 7.2 Avoid / 避免事项

**English — execution reference**

Avoid generic startup or SaaS styling, excessive neon cyberpunk aesthetics, horror-style dreamcore, meaningless animation, over-designed interfaces, effects without a storytelling purpose, inaccessible interactions, and visual treatment that competes with the original work.

**中文 — 创作说明**

避免通用创业公司 / SaaS 风格、过量霓虹赛博朋克、恐怖化的 dreamcore、没有意义的动画、过度设计的界面、脱离叙事目的的特效、无法正常访问的交互，以及抢走原创作品注意力的视觉包装。

### 7.3 Dreamcore and Glitch / Dreamcore 与 Glitch 的定义

**English — execution reference**

Dreamcore means quiet, empty, memory-like, soft, and slightly unreal. It must not become horror, heavy cyberpunk, arbitrary surrealism, excessive neon, or visual chaos.

Glitch is a narrative device. Use it to express fragmentation, memory, identity, error, or transition. Do not apply random distortion throughout the interface.

Motion and interaction must clarify hierarchy, reveal content, create narrative pacing, support navigation, or reinforce the project's concept. Every major effect should have a stated purpose and a reduced-motion alternative.

**中文 — 创作说明**

这里的 dreamcore 指安静、留白、像记忆一样、柔和、略微不真实，而不是恐怖、重赛博朋克、随机超现实、过量霓虹或视觉混乱。Glitch 是表达碎片、记忆、身份、错误或转场的叙事工具，不是到处随机扭曲。

动画与互动应该帮助理解层级、揭示内容、控制叙事节奏、支持导航或支持项目概念。重要效果都要能说明“为什么存在”，并为减少动态效果的用户准备替代方案。

## 8. Bilingual Content Rules / 双语内容规则

**English — execution reference**

English and Chinese must cover the same factual content and project status. Translation may adapt tone and sentence structure; it does not need to be literal. English should be concise, clear, and suitable for admissions and professional review. Chinese should preserve creative nuance and support personal review without introducing conflicting facts. Project titles may retain intentional English naming, with a Chinese explanation where useful. Every future content or scope update must be made in both languages in the same revision unless explicitly exempted by the owner.

**中文 — 创作说明**

中英文必须保持事实、范围与项目状态一致。翻译可以根据语言习惯调整语气与句式，不必逐字对应。英文要简洁、清楚，适合申请与专业评审。中文要保留创作细节并方便个人审阅，但不能增加与英文冲突的事实。有意使用的英文项目名可以保留，必要时补充中文解释。未来每次修改内容或范围，都要在同一轮更新中同步两种语言，除非作者明确允许例外。

## 9. Process Documentation Requirements / 过程记录要求

**English — execution reference**

Every project must document both the final output and the process. At minimum, collect and curate relevant evidence from sketches, moodboards, research notes, wireframes, experiments, failed attempts, revisions, technical development, decision-making, supplier communication where relevant, content funnels where relevant, and final evaluation.

Process evidence must explain decisions rather than merely display artifacts. Captions should answer one or more of the following: What was tested? Why was it tested? What changed? What was learned? How did the result affect the next decision?

**中文 — 创作说明**

每个项目都必须同时展示最终成果和过程。可用材料包括草图、moodboard、研究笔记、线框、实验、失败尝试、修改、技术开发、决策记录、相关供应商沟通、相关内容漏斗与最终评估。

过程页不能只是把材料排出来；说明文字要回答：测试了什么、为什么测试、哪里发生了变化、学到了什么，以及这一步如何影响下一次决定。过程本身就是创意思考能力的证据。

## 10. Design and Development Workflow / 设计与开发流程

**English — execution reference**

The existing repository baseline uses **Vite, React, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS**. Future implementation should preserve this stack and its working architecture unless the owner explicitly approves a migration. Maintain clear separation among components, pages, layouts, data, styles, documentation, original final assets, process assets, and exports.

Neutral placeholders may be used while original materials are being prepared, but they must be clearly identifiable and must never imply that unfinished or unavailable work is complete. Final public-facing site copy should support English and Chinese as defined in Section 8; internal planning notes may use Chinese when that improves speed and accuracy.

Preferred responsibilities:

- **Figma:** visual direction, storyboard, wireframes, layout, interaction planning, and reviewable prototypes.
- **Codex:** implementation, React/Tailwind development, reusable components, responsive behavior, accessibility, testing, and iteration.
- **Human decision:** concept, aesthetics, original content, content selection, storytelling, ethical judgment, and final approval.

Preferred sequence:

1. Confirm concept and content inventory.
2. Define information architecture and bilingual content model.
3. Create the hero Figma storyboard and project brief updates.
4. Design wireframes and visual system in Figma.
5. Review and approve the core direction.
6. Implement with Codex.
7. Review content, interactions, responsiveness, and accessibility.
8. Iterate and document decisions.

Implementation must not begin until the pre-coding decisions in Section 12 have been reviewed.

**中文 — 创作说明**

现有仓库的技术基础是 **Vite、React、TypeScript 与 Tailwind CSS**。除非我明确批准迁移，后续实现应保留这套技术与已有的有效架构。组件、页面、布局、数据、样式、文档、原创最终资产、过程资产和导出文件之间要保持清楚区分。

原创材料尚未准备好时可以使用中性占位内容，但必须清楚标识，不能让未完成或不存在的作品看起来像最终成果。面对公众的网站文案按第 8 节支持中英文；内部规划笔记在有利于速度和准确性时可以使用中文。

Figma 主要负责视觉方向、storyboard、线框、布局、互动规划与可审阅原型；Codex 负责 React/Tailwind 实现、可复用组件、响应式、可访问性、测试与迭代；我本人负责概念、美学、原创内容、选材、叙事、伦理判断和最终确认。

建议流程是：确认概念与素材清单 -> 定义信息架构和双语内容模型 -> 绘制首屏 Figma storyboard 并更新项目 brief -> 在 Figma 设计线框与视觉系统 -> 审核核心方向 -> Codex 实现 -> 检查内容、互动、响应式和可访问性 -> 迭代并记录决定。在第 12 节的开发前事项确认之前，不进入网站编码。

## 11. Quality and Accessibility Requirements / 质量与可访问性要求

**English — execution reference**

The implemented site should eventually meet the following baseline: responsive layouts for mobile, tablet, and desktop; semantic navigation and heading hierarchy; keyboard-accessible controls and visible focus states; meaningful alternative text or captions for portfolio media; readable color contrast and typography; reduced-motion support; clear bilingual controls and persistent language behavior; optimized media that preserves appropriate visual quality; clear disclosure for video, audio, or interaction requirements; and graceful fallback when advanced effects or interactive media are unavailable.

These requirements should protect the work's clarity rather than force a generic appearance.

**中文 — 创作说明**

未来实现至少要支持手机、平板和桌面端，具备语义清楚的导航与标题层级、键盘操作和可见焦点，为作品图片提供有意义的替代文字或说明，并保证色彩对比与文字可读性。

网站还要支持减少动态效果、明确且可持续的语言选择、合理优化媒体文件，并在高级效果或互动媒体无法运行时提供稳妥的替代内容。这些要求是为了保护作品的清晰度，而不是把视觉做成通用模板。
