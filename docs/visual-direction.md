# Visual Direction Notes

Working direction:

- editorial
- image-forward
- quiet archive
- lightly experimental
- not SaaS
- not generic startup minimalism

Current scaffold decisions:

- warm paper-like background instead of flat white
- serif-led headings to support an editorial tone
- soft panel borders and grid textures instead of glossy cards
- restrained motion for now
- space reserved for original screenshots, photographs, scans, and process records

Possible future references to explore through your own material:

- dreamlike archive
- fragmented interface
- glitch residue
- digital memory
- cultural trace

Guardrails:

- no stock photos
- no fake client outcomes
- no AI-generated final artwork in place of your own portfolio work
- no overdesigned animation before content is stable
